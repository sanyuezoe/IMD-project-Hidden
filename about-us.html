About the project

How it all started

Context

During the Covid-19 pandemic, billions of people around the world were in some type of lockdown. Women and girls are more likely to be victims of domestic violence than ever before, no matter where they are in the world.

Concept

Establish a virtual space that engages people with what happened

Goals

Raise public awareness about domestic abuse, cultivate empathy as a starting point for change.
